
[[File:802 Theon & Alys.png|thumb|right|Alys alongside Theon Greyjoy during the war council.]]Alys arrives at [[Winterfell]], not long after Jon returns with Daenerys and the Targaryen army, with the Karstark forces from Karhold. Lord [[Yohn Royce]] meets her and escorts her in Winterfell.<ref name="E68">"[[Winterfell (episode)|Winterfell]]"</ref>

Alys is present for the trial of [[Jaime Lannister|Jaime]], sitting alongside the other Northern lords. She is also present for the war council in preparation for the upcoming battle with the [[Wights|army of the dead]].<ref>"[[A Knight of the Seven Kingdoms]]"</ref>

[[File:803 Ironborn Karstark Bran.png|thumb|left|Alys and Theon Greyjoy escort Bran to the godswood.]]
During the [[Battle of Winterfell (Great War)|Battle of Winterfell]], Alys and a group of Karstark archers join [[Theon Greyjoy|Theon]] and the [[ironborn]] in defending [[Bran Stark|Bran]]. She is killed when the wights overrun the godswood. Her death renders House Karstark extinct.<ref>"[[The Long Night]]"</ref>
