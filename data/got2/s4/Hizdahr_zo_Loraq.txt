His father is crucified in the aftermath of the siege, a matter which he later confronts her about personally. Some weeks later, Daenerys tells [[Jorah Mormont]] to send Hizdahr to [[Yunkai]] as her ambassador. Hizdahr is to offer the [[Wise Masters]] a choice: they can "live in her new world" and accept the abolition of slavery, or "die in their old one" at the hands of the [[Second Sons]] sent under the command of [[Daario Naharis]].<ref>"[[Mockingbird]]"</ref>
